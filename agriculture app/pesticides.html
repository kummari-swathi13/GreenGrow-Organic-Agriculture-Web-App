Natural & Organic Pesticides

Organic pesticides are eco-friendly, biodegradable, and safe for the environment, farmers, and consumers. Here is an in-depth guide to commonly used organic pesticides, their preparation, usage, and targeted pests.

1. Neem Oil

Target Pests: Aphids, whiteflies, mealybugs, caterpillars

Preparation: Mix 2 tablespoons of neem oil with 1 liter of warm water and a few drops of liquid soap. Shake well before spraying.

  • Acts as a repellent, growth inhibitor, and egg-laying deterrent
  • Apply every 7â€“10 days on early morning or evening
  • Safe for beneficial insects when applied properly

2. Garlic-Chili Spray

Target Pests: Beetles, mites, ants, armyworms

Preparation: Blend 1 bulb of garlic, 2â€“3 hot chilies, and 1 onion with 1 liter of water. Let sit overnight, then strain and spray.

  • Natural insect repellent with antibacterial properties
  • Can be stored in the refrigerator up to 1 week
  • Use protective gloves while spraying

3. Tobacco Decoction

Target Pests: Leafhoppers, thrips, aphids

Preparation: Soak 50g of tobacco leaves in 1 liter of water overnight. Add soap before application.

  • Contains nicotine which disrupts pests' nervous systems
  • Do not use on edible leafy greens near harvest
  • Use sparingly as it is a strong pesticide

4. Cow Urine Spray

Target Pests: Scale insects, fungal infections

Preparation: Mix 1 liter of fermented cow urine with 10 liters of water. Optionally add neem leaf extract.

  • Boosts plant immunity
  • Also serves as a foliar fertilizer
  • Use within 3â€“4 days of preparation

5. Ash + Buttermilk Mixture

Target Pests: Mites, powdery mildew

Preparation: Mix equal parts wood ash and buttermilk. Let ferment for 24 hours. Strain before spraying.

  • Effective against fungal spores
  • Should be used weekly for persistent infections
  • Apply during dry weather only

6. Soap Solution

Target Pests: Soft-bodied insects like whiteflies, aphids

Preparation: Mix 1 tablespoon of mild liquid soap (not detergent) in 1 liter of water. Spray directly on pests.

  • Works by suffocating insects
  • Test on small leaf area before full application
